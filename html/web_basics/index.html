Depending on where you live, Japanese ramen noodles have been gaining popularity in recent years. This inexpensive and simple meal has been recreated with different ingredients that make it more exciting and delicious to eat. Some styles aren't for everyone so here is some information to help choose which noodles to slurp!

The most popular noodle style is ramen. Ramen noodles are thin, made from wheat and served in a broth. Restaurants may offer choices of noodle thickness. Ramen examples include:

  • Shoyu
  • Shio
  • Miso
  • Tonkotsu
  • Curry

Other noodle styles include the following:

  1. Shirataki
  2. Soba (personal favorite)
  3. Somen
  4. Udon
